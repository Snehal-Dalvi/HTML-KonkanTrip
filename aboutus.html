Get to Know Us

Our Mission

At Konkan Trips, our goal is to make your Konkan getaway seamless, joyful, and unforgettable. We hand-pick local experiences, ensure comfortable stays, and take pride in showcasing the culture, nature, and flavors of coastal Maharashtra.

Why Choose Us?

Whether you're craving serene beaches, forts with a story, or just delicious seafood, our 2D/1N plans are made for the perfect escape. With budget, deluxe, and premium packages, we have something for everyone. Our local guides and curated itineraries make all the difference.
